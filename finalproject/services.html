Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance for airport transfers and 48 hours for private tours to ensure availability.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 24 hours before the scheduled service receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours incur a 50% charge.

Do you provide child seats?

Yes, we offer child seats at no additional cost. Please specify your requirements when booking.

Are your drivers certified?

All our chauffeurs are professionally licensed, certified, and undergo regular training to maintain our high standards.

What areas do you serve?

We serve all of Cape Town and surrounding areas, including the Winelands, Cape Peninsula, and airport transfers.

Can I request a specific vehicle?

Yes, you can request a specific vehicle type when booking, subject to availability.
