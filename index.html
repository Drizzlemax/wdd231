About Me

Hello! I'm Desmond Chibanda, though many know me as Linx. Originally from Zimbabwe, I now call the vibrant city of Cape Town, South Africa, my home. By profession, I navigate the streets as an Uber driver, a role that perfectly complements my deep passions for connecting with people, exploring new places, and embracing diverse cultures. Beyond the wheel, I am a dedicated family man and a lifelong learner with a growing fascination for the digital world, particularly web development. I am constantly seeking out new skills and knowledge, driven by a belief in continuous personal growth and the power of technology to create opportunities.

Looking to the future, my ambition extends far beyond the driver's seat. I am actively building my expertise in web development with a clear and purposeful goal: to launch a startup dedicated to empowering small businesses across Africa. I envision creating accessible, affordable digital tools and services—from e-commerce platforms to digital marketing strategies—that will help local entrepreneurs establish a strong online presence, reach wider markets, and thrive in a competitive economy. My ultimate mission is to leverage technology to foster economic growth, create jobs, and contribute to a more prosperous and connected African community.
